      3. Circle:Move only one space in each of the four directions: east, west, south, and north.
      Line:Move only two spaces in each of the four directions: east, west, south, and north.
      Triangle: Move only three spaces diagonally: east, west, south, and north.
      Square: Moves only four spaces in the four directions: east, west, south, and north.
      Pentagon:Moves only five spaces in the four directions: east, west, south, and north.
      Hexagon:Hexagon: Move six spaces in each of the four directions: east, west, south, and north.
    A.You can't pass through a blocked area.
    B.Each shape must fill its own number of movement spaces.
    C.Each shape gets points equal to the number of moves it has.
    D.When each shape touches another without a gap, it is deformed due to the score difference
    and the shape with the lower score disappears.
    E.The side with the higher score takes the disappeared shape and obtains the score for that shape.
